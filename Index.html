Biography

I am Atanda Oluwatimileyin, an undergraduate student at the Federal University of Technology Akure (FUTA). My journey into web development began in 2021 with an introduction to HTML and CSS. Initially, it was a casual exploration, but it didn't go too far because I thought it was too hard for me to learn. However, a recent educational experience at Alt School, which began three months ago, reignited and deepened my passion for frontend development.

As I delve into the intricacies of coding and design, I have found a new love for creating interactive and visually appealing web experiences. Even though I am on the Cloud Engineering Track with the Student ID ALT/SOE/024/0160, I still love frontend development.

Beyond my academic and professional pursuits, I enjoy reading books, which offers me a diverse range of insights and inspiration. My journey as a budding developer is marked by a commitment to continuous learning and a growing enthusiasm for the world of coding.

Why AltSchool

I wanted a platform that allows me to take a self-paced course in tech while keeping me committed through weekly interactions and structured examinations. AltSchool provided more by introducing Learning Circles and other useful skills like technical writing. I might not have had so much reason before choosing Alt School, but I don't regret that decision.
